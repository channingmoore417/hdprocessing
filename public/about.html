Our Story

We Saw How Lafayette Businesses Were Getting Burned.

HD Processing was founded in Lafayette in 2019 after watching too many Acadiana business owners get hit with rate increases they never agreed to, contracts nobody explained, and customer service lines that went to voicemail. The payment processing industry had a reputation problem — and it was earned.

We built HD Processing to be the opposite. Local reps who show up in person. Pricing explained line by line before you sign anything. Month-to-month terms because we want to earn your business every single month — not lock you in and coast.

Today we serve over 500 businesses across Acadiana — from restaurants on Jefferson Street to contractors in the oil field, salons on the Northside to e-commerce brands shipping across the country. Same deal for everyone: honest rates, modern equipment, and a local team that picks up the phone.
